VaultGuard Privacy Policy

Effective Date: September 1, 2025 | Last Updated: September 1, 2025

1. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

Account Information:

  • Username (chosen by you)
  • Password hash (bcrypt encrypted)
  • Encryption salt (for your password encryption)

Vault Data:

  • Site names and usernames (encrypted)
  • Passwords (AES-256 encrypted)
  • Timestamps for security auditing

Security Logs:

  • Login attempts and times
  • IP addresses for security monitoring
  • Failed authentication attempts

2. HOW WE USE INFORMATION

  • Password Storage: Your passwords are encrypted before storage
  • Security Monitoring: Detecting and preventing unauthorized access
  • Service Improvement: Anonymous usage statistics
  • Legal Compliance: Required security auditing

3. ZERO-KNOWLEDGE ARCHITECTURE

  • We cannot decrypt your stored passwords
  • Your master password is never stored
  • Encryption happens client-side before transmission

4. DATA SHARING

We DO NOT:

  • Sell your personal information
  • Share passwords with third parties
  • Provide data to advertisers
  • Use your passwords for any purpose

We MAY share information only:

  • When required by law
  • To protect our rights or safety
  • With your explicit consent

5. DATA SECURITY

Technical Safeguards:

  • AES-256 encryption for password storage
  • HTTPS for data transmission
  • PBKDF2 key derivation (600,000 iterations)
  • Security event logging
  • Account lockout protection

Administrative Safeguards:

  • Limited access to systems
  • Security incident procedures
  • Regular security reviews

6. YOUR RIGHTS

You have the right to:

  • Access your account information
  • Delete your account and data
  • Export your data (encrypted format)
  • Request security incident information

7. DATA RETENTION

  • Active accounts: Data retained indefinitely
  • Deleted accounts: Data purged within 30 days
  • Security logs: Retained for 1 year

8. INTERNATIONAL USERS

If you access VaultGuard from outside your jurisdiction, you are responsible for compliance with local laws.

9. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

VaultGuard is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

10. CONTACT INFORMATION

For privacy concerns or questions:

  • Review our security documentation
  • Contact through official channels only
  • Report security issues immediately

For privacy questions, please contact: privacy@vaultguard.local

Security contact: security@vaultguard.local
